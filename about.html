Background

For many years, Miki has been fascinated by fantasy, science fiction and other geeky interests. He started drawing from an early age, and with the introduction to manga and anime, he developed his own style.
Professionally, he designs characters for learning oriented videos at the University of Copenhagen. In his spare time, he takes up worldbuilding, and a webcomic taking place in one such fantasy world might be underway as well.
